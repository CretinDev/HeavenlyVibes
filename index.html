About Me

My name is Jim Craven. Welcome to my page.

I am multi media image artist based out of Ontario Canada. I specialize in memento style candid portraiture at media events and private gatherings. Whether its a wedding, proposal, celebration of life, birthday, red carpet event, or somebody's personalized vision or idea. I aim to capture precious moments and pictures that people will look back on and cherish in the years to come

I provide 35mm film photos and portraits, 3d stereoscopic Film Photos, Drone Imagery, and occasionally digital, (usually vertical) video. Service comes with basic color accurate grading and video editing by yours truly.

For bookings, collaborations or idea sharing, feel free to reach out.
